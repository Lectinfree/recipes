Cauliflower pizza recipe (Lectin Free Pizza)üòãüçï



This is my favorite cauliflower pizza recipe. A simple recipe with just 3 ingredients!

You can use fresh or frozen cauliflower in this recipe (to save time). You can also double the recipe so that you have a frozen pizza ready for a busy evening!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cauliflower
  • 1 egg
  • pinch of salt
  • pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 cup parmigiano reggiano
  • 2 cloves of garlic
Topping:
  • 3 cups mushrooms
  • pinch of salt
  • pepper
  • oregano
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • olive oil
  • 1 cup of buffalo mozzarella


Directions:

Cut the cauliflower into small pieces and grind it in a blender.
Put the cauliflower rice in the microwave for 5 minutes and then, when cooled, strain it well through a thin mesh bag or cloth.
cauliflower rice Add egg, salt, pepper, oregano, garlic and parmigiano to cauliflower rice.
Mix it all well.
On a baking tray or on a stone to bake a pizza, spread the cauliflower rice in a circle, with a slightly thickened edge and bake for 25 minutes at 180C | 355F.
While the cauliflower rice is in the oven, slice the mushrooms, salt them, add the pepper, oregano, garlic and olive oil and mix everything well.
After 25 minutes, take the pizza out of the oven and put the mozzarella and mushrooms on it.
Bake everything together for another 10 minutes, 180C | 355F.
You can add fresh basil before serving.

Broccoli Flatbread



Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw broccoli rice
  • 1 cup almond flour / ground almonds
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of dried herbs of choice (oregano, thyme, lemon pepper), optional
  • Sea salt and black pepper for the taste

Directions:

Use your hands to pull the dry ingredients towards the middle until everything is combined andyou can shape it into a ball.
Transfer to the baking paper and form into a rectangular base by flattening the dough with your hands.

Cauliflower Shepherd's Pie


For a truly lectin-free filling, use LEMON juice !!!!

Ingredients:

  • Minced (meat) quorn
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.1 lbs (500gr) quorn (minced)
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock
  • salt and pepper to taste
Topping:
  • 1 cauliflower
  • 1.7 oz (50 ml) coconut cream
  • 0.11lbs (50gr) grated cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

Cut the onion and carrot into small pieces and crush the garlic.
Heat the oil in a pan and add the onion and garlic.
Then add the sliced carrot and fry it for a few minutes.
Add the quorn, tomato puree, vegetable stock, salt, pepper and the bay leaf and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
Now preheat the oven to 175 degrees.
Cut the cauliflower into florets.
Boil the florets for 10 minutes.
Drain the water from the pan and drain the florets well, then return the drained florets to the panand add the coconut cream, cheese, salt and pepper and mix with a hand blender until smooth.
Divide the quorn over an oven dish.
Spoon the cauliflower puree over this and then divide the puree evenly over the dish.
Sprinkle the dish with a little cheese
Bake the Shepherds pie for 25 minutes.

SWEET POTATO GNOCHI


SOFT SWEET POTATO GNOCCHI ARESERVED WITH BUTTER AND SAGE.

Ingredients:

  • 4 SWEET POTATOES
  • 2 CUPS ALMOND FLOUR, PLUS MORE ASNEEDED
  • 1 CUP ARROWROOT POWDER, PLUS MOREAS NEEDED
  • 1 TSP SALT
  • 1 EGG
  • 1/2 CUP BUTTER
  • SAGE LEAVES

Directions:

PIERCE THE SWEET POTATO WITH A FORK SEVERAL TIMES.
MICROWAVE THE SWEET POTATO FOR 10 MINUTES, OR UNTIL THE INSIDE IS SOFT.
REMOVE THE PULP FROM THE SWEET POTATO AND PUT IT IN A POTATO RICER, LET IT COOL FOR 10 MINUTES.
ADD THE ALMOND FLOUR, ARROWROOT FLOUR, SALT AND EGG AND MIX UNTIL A THICK PASTE FORMS.
IF THE MIXTURE ISTOO STICKY, ADD MORE FLOUR UNTILYOU CAN FORM A BALL WITH THEDOUGH.
CUT THE DOUGH INTO PARTS.
ROLL UP EACH PIECE AND CUT IT INTOSMALL PIECES.
BRING A LARGE POT OF SALTED WATER TO A BOIL.
ADD THE GNOCCHI TO THE PAN ANDCOOK FOR 2-3 MINUTES.
PUT THE BUTTER AND SAGE IN A DRYPAN AND ADD THE GNOCCHI WHEN SAGEHAS BECOME CRISPY.
